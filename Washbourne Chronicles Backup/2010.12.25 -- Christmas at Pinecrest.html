Three lovely days around Christmas in 2010 at Pinecrest and Dodge Ridge ski area near Yosemite.

Believe it or not, this is a close as I (John) have ever been to Yosemite even though I have spent 15+ years in California (I plan to fix that in 2011).

We enjoyed two perfect days of skiing and on the final night 6 inches of fresh powder was dumped for sledding enjoyment on the way back to the bay area.

2010 was the year that Katerina learned to ski. I remembered to bring the "edgie wedgies" and that made all the difference (see the orange things between the skis on the picture below). We have another trip planned in February and I think she will be ready for the black diamond runs with her brothers.
