We got the bug for a little backpacking in November and decided to go to Pt Reyes. Pt Reyes is not backcountry camping because there are pit toilets and potable water, but the destination is beautiful and I admit it is pretty nice not to have to dig a hole to go to the bathroom.

We spent two nights, one just off the water in Wildcat Camp, and one up on the ridge in Sky Camp. We parked at the Bear Valley Visitor Center (park headquarters), and had a 7 mile day down to Wildcat Camp on the coast, an 8 mile day past Arch Rock and back up to Sky Camp, and finally a 4 mile route over Mt Whittenburg and back out to the trailhead.

We had absolutely perfect weather, so nice that I plan to return every November that I can, and may even try January.
